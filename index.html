Frequently Asked Questions

What print formats do you offer?

We offer three premium formats to suit different aesthetics and spaces:

  • Canvas Prints: Traditional fine art feel with textured surface, perfect for classic and transitional decor
  • Metal Prints: Ultra-modern with vibrant colors and glossy finish, ideal for contemporary spaces
  • Framed Prints: Fine art paper in professional frames, classic gallery presentation

All formats use museum-quality materials and professional printing processes.

How do I choose the right size for my space?

For best visual impact, the print should occupy 50-75% of your available wall width:

  • Small walls (4-6 feet wide): 24x16" or 36x24"
  • Medium walls (6-8 feet wide): 36x24" or 48x32"
  • Large walls (8+ feet wide): 48x32" or 60x40"
  • Above furniture: Print should be approximately 2/3 the width of the furniture below

Each product page includes detailed sizing guidance specific to that image.

Do you offer a satisfaction guarantee?

Yes! We stand behind the quality of our prints. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 30 days for a full refund. We want you to love the art in your space.
